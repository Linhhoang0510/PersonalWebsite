About Me

I'm a Data Analyst & Developer with a Master's degree in Data Science and proven expertise in delivering scalable, value-driven data products from concept through deployment and continuous optimization. I specialize in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights that drive strategic business decisions and measurable outcomes.

With expert-level Power BI skills including advanced DAX, dynamic reporting, custom visuals, and performance tuning, I have a strong track record of creating interactive dashboards that directly inform sales, marketing, and operational strategies. My experience spans multi-source data integration, ETL pipeline automation, cloud platforms (AWS, Azure), and data governance frameworks.

I thrive in Agile environments and excel at collaborating with stakeholders at all levels, translating complex business requirements into high-impact reporting solutions. My passion lies in leveraging data to solve real-world problems, optimize processes, and unlock new growth opportunities.
